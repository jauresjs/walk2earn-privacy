Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

Data We Collect

Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and a password (stored securely via Supabase Auth).

Step Data: With your permission, we read step count data from your device's pedometer. This data never leaves your device unless you are signed in — we store only the aggregated count (not raw sensor data).

No Health Data Sharing: Your step data is never sold or shared with third parties. It is used solely to calculate your rewards.

How We Use Data

• To display your step count and earnings in the app

• To calculate and distribute rewards

• To improve the app experience

Data Storage

Your account data is stored securely via Supabase (hosted on AWS). Step counts are stored as aggregated daily totals — we never store step-by-step location data.

Third-Party Services

We use Supabase for authentication and database hosting. Supabase processes your data under their own privacy policy.

Your Rights

You can delete your account at any time from the Rewards tab in the app, or by contacting us. Your data is yours — we don't sell it.

Children's Privacy

Walk2Earn is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Contact

For privacy concerns or account deletion, contact: rredemption99@gmail.com
